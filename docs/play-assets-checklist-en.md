# Play Assets Checklist (EN)

Prepare these assets before submitting closed testing:

- App icon: 512x512 PNG
- Feature graphic: 1024x500 PNG
- Phone screenshots: at least 2 (recommended 4-8)
- Optional: 7-inch / 10-inch tablet screenshots (if tablet support is claimed)
- Privacy policy public URL (HTTPS)

Recommended screenshot flow:
1. Home role selection
2. Organizer dashboard
3. Event creation
4. Participant search
5. Registration form
6. Payment step
